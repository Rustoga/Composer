About This Portfolio

This collection represents my journey as a composer and sound designer, spanning from commercial client work to personal artistic exploration. Each project has contributed to developing my distinctive approach to sound creation—blending commercial sensibility with creative experimentation.

Whether composing for film, designing interactive audio for games, or exploring electroacoustic territories, I approach each project with the same commitment to quality and innovation. My work has been featured in various media, from streaming platforms to independent game projects.
