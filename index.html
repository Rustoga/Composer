About

Rudolf Westerholm is a multidisciplinary composer with almost two decades of experience from the fields of music and sound.
He is a practising professional in the video game industry and has acquired a Master of Sound degree from Aalto University, Finland with minor studies in Sibelius Academy on Game & Music Technology.

Rudolf's compositions tie together both experimental sound design and melodic components, drawing inspiration from utilizing scientific research of cross-disciplinary fields into conveying desired emotions musically. These intricacies of sound and variety of technique can best be heard in the discography of his solo artist project Rustoga and read through his publication Making video game music more accessible for visually impaired.
