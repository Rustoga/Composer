About

Rudolf 'Rustoga' Westerholm is a Finnish multi-genre composer, singer and drummer.
Background as a Sound Designer in Housemarque (Latest project Returnal) and with an artist name Rustoga since 2011.

Mellow but warm atmospheres are a combining factor along with technical approach to sound.

Master of Sound from Aalto University, Minor of Music Technology in Sibelius Academy on game and electronic music composition. His Master's Master's Thesis 'Making video game music more accessible for visually impaired' has gotten good reception as the thesis connects video game design into accessible implementation in the composing phase, and now is creating a conference paper with a small research team on the subject.
