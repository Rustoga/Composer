About

Rudolf Westerholm is a multidisciplinary composer with almost two decades of experience in music and sound. He is a practising professional in the video game industry and holds a Master of Sound degree from Aalto University, Finland, with minor studies in Sibelius Academy on Game & Music Technology.

His compositions blend experimental sound design with melodic elements, drawing on cross‑disciplinary research to evoke emotion. These qualities are showcased in his solo project Rustoga and in his publication Making video game music more accessible for visually impaired.
