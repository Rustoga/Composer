About

Rudolf Westerholm is a multidisciplinary composer with almost two decades of experience in music and sound. He is a practising professional in the video game industry and holds a Master of Sound degree from Aalto University, Finland, and Academic minor on Game Music & Music Technology from Sibelius Academy.

His compositions blend experimental sound design with melodic elements, drawing on cross‑disciplinary research to evoke emotion. These qualities are showcased in his solo project Rustoga and in his publication Making Video Game Music More Accessible For Visually Impaired.

Selected Works

  • Soundtrack work for YLE’s documentary series exploring Nordic landscapes and identity.

  • Original compositions and immersive sound design for Housemarque’s sci-fi action titles.

  • Collaborative sound installations and product compositions for academic research at Aalto.

  • Crafted brand-defining theme music for Vacuum Wood’s tech-forward identity.

  • Energetic and memorable audio branding for HypeWorks’ digital campaigns.
